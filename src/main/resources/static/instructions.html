Instructions

Making a Card

Use any medium you want to make a card. It can be a drawing, a letter, or even a few nice words. You don't have to be Picasso or Shakespeare to send a touching message - it's the thought that counts!

Submitting

Go to our Submit link in the header and fill out the fields on the page!

Guidelines

1. Please avoid any sort of profanity, vulgarity, or otherwise obscene or inflammatory content in your cards. After all, this site is meant to be a hub for positivity :)

2. While your cards can target specific groups, we encourage you to also submit cards that everyone can enjoy!

3. Try to avoid mentioning any depressing events, news, or ideas in your card; rather, emphasize the good moments and ideas that would leave your readers happier than before they saw your card.
